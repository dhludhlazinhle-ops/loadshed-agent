# Load Shed Smart - SA Productivity Agent

> An AI agent that helps South Africans stay productive during load shedding.

Built for the Google Cloud Rapid Agent Hackathon 2026 - MongoDB Track

---

## The Problem

Every day, millions of South Africans lose hours of productivity to load shedding.
In townships like Kwa-Thema, Springs and Benoni, the problem is even worse —
cable theft causes unplanned blackouts that Eskom does not warn you about.
Communities have no easy way to share power-related information with each other.

Load Shed Smart solves all of this with one AI agent.

---

## What It Does

### Personal AI Agent
- Remembers your name and area permanently — no setup every time
- Shows your personalized morning briefing with load shedding times for your area
- You chat naturally about your tasks and schedule
- AI detects conflicts between your tasks and power outages
- Suggests the best alternative times to get things done

### Community Board
- Any resident can report cable theft with exact location
- Report transformer faults and unplanned outages
- Councillors post official updates visible to the whole community
- Community shares news when power is restored
- All reports stored by area so only your community sees your reports
